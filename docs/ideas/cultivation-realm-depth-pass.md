> **Current priority (owner):** Flesh out **existing major cultivation realms** with depth — power, scale, capabilities — before continuing Chaos / post-immortal design. Endgame ideas are **parked** until the lower ladder feels like a real foundation.

## Intent

You can’t size Chaos, false immortality, or upper realms against a shallow seven-realm climb. This pass is **not** “add realm 8 tomorrow” — it’s making each **current** watershed feel like a distinct tier of what a cultivator **is** and **can do**, so later design has something to push against.

## Scope (draft)

For each major realm (qi / body / spirit tracks as applicable):

- **Power & scale** — what they can destroy, endure, sense, affect (not just +stats)
- **Capabilities** — unique tools unlocked (sense, flight?, domain?, lifespan band, social standing)
- **Identity** — how NPCs/world treats someone *at peak* vs *just entered*
- **Time cost** — tie to [`watershed-realms-lifespan-pacing.md`](watershed-realms-lifespan-pacing.md) when ready
- **Breakthrough feel** — tie to [`tribulation-per-realm-limbo.md`](tribulation-per-realm-limbo.md)
